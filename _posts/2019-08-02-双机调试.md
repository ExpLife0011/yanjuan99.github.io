### 虚拟机设置
如下图设置串行端口
<img src="/images/B/3.gif" height="800" width="850">

> 1. bcdedit /copy {current} /d “Win10 copy”
> 2. bcdedit /timeout 30
> 3. 重启 进启动项 Win10 copy
> 4. bcdedit /debug ON 
> 5. bcdedit /bootdebug ON
> 6. bcdedit /dbgsettings serial debugport:1 baudrate:115200
